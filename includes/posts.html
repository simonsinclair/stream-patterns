Headline

Flood defence projects will share £2.3bn

Body copy

More than 1,400 flood defence projects are to receive funding to protect 300,00 homes, the Treasury has said.

Inline link

The plan also includes a £1bn scheme to build the world's first power-generating tidal lagoon in Swansea Bay.

Unordered list

Major projects to be allocated cash include the:

  • Thames Estuary, being given £196m
  • Humber Estuary, given £80m
  • Lincolnshire (£73m)
  • Rossall in Lancashire (£47m)
  • Oxford (£42m)
  • Tonbridge area (£17m)
